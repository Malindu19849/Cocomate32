Efficiency | Safety | Productivity

Our project's goal is to make coconut harvesting easier and safer by using machines to help with the work. This will help farmers get the job done faster, lower labor costs, and reduce the risks associated with manual harvesting. By making the process more efficient and safe, we aim to improve the overall productivity and well-being of those involved in coconut farming.
